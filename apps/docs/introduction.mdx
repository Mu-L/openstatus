---
title: What is OpenStatus?
---

OpenStatus is an open-source synthetic and frontend performance monitoring
service.

## Products

OpenStatus is composed of two products:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Synthetic Monitoring" href="/synthetic">
    Monitor your website or API availability and
    performance from different locations around the world.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real User Monitoring" href="/rum">
    Monitor your website performance from your users' browsers.
  </Card>

</CardGroup>

## Trying OpenStatus

The easiest way to quickly try the app is to signup on
[www.openstatus.dev](https://www.openstatus.dev)

The signup is free.

## Getting Started

There are three ways to get started with OpenStatus.

- **Cloud**: the fastest and easiest way to try the app (it's free)
- **Local**: if you're a developer and would like to experiment or contribute to
  the app
- **Self-hosting**: if you want greater control over your data and want to run
  the app on your own server
